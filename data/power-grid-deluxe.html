North America

Setup
  1. Shuffle dark plants & remove per table above
  2. Take 8 dark plants & place them face up, sort high to low
  3. Take 1 dark plant and set it aside with the step 3 card
  4. Shuffle together all remaining light & dark plant cards (Except the 1 set aside with the step 3 card)
  5. Place step 3 card on the bottom of plant deck and the dark plant on top of the plant deck
Starting Resources
North America
Coal Spaces 2-9
Natural Gas Spaces 3-8
Oil Spaces 4-9
Uranium Space 9
Plant Market Rule:

At the beginning of each Phase 2 (Auction Plants) (entire game), place the "1" discount marker on the lowest plant. Starting bid for this plant is "1". If nobody buys this plant, it is removed from the game. If a lower plant is drawn as a replacement for a purchased plant, then the "1" discount is removed for this round.

Europe

Setup
  1. Shuffle dark plants & remove per table above
  2. Take 9 dark plants & place them face up, sort high to low
  3. Shuffle together all remaining light & dark plant cards
  4. Place step 3 card on the bottom of plant deck
Starting Resources
Europe
Coal Spaces 2-9
Natural Gas Spaces 3-8
Oil Spaces 3-9
Uranium Space 8-9
Plant Market Rule:

If all players opt out of the auction and no plant is sold, then remove the lowest plant from the game and replace it

Step 2

Step 2 (2 players per city) starts at the beginning of Phase 5 (Bureaucracy) North America: Remove the lowest plant from the market & replace it Europe: Remove the lowest plant from the market & do not replace it

Step 3

Step 3 (3 players per city) starts depending upon when drawn from the plant deck:

Phase 2 (Auction) Step 3 card is now the highest plant. After Phase 2, remove step 3 card & lowest plant, shuffle plant deck. Step 3 starts in Phase 3 (Buy Resources). 
Phase 4 (Build) Remove step 3 card & lowest plant, shuffle plant deck. Step 3 starts at beginning of Phase 5 (Bureaucracy). 
Phase 5 (Bureaucracy) Remove step 3 card & lowest plant, shuffle plant deck. Refill resources for Step 2 then start next round in Step 3
